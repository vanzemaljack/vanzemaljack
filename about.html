About Me
Hi, I am a Highliner and a third-year student majoring in Computer Science at the Faculty of Mathematics, Astronomy, and Physics (FAMAF). Throughout my educational journey, I have explored various aspects of programming, including a year of self-directed learning that led me to delve into Full-Stack development. Although I initially focused on creating websites, I soon discovered that my true passion for programming could be found in any field, which motivated me to enroll in college. I possess a strong capacity for understanding and adaptation. I enjoy challenging problems and the various ways in which they can be solved.
